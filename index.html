Okoa Maisha

100% of your donations goes to paying of medical bills for needy families.

All Lives Matter




16.9% of Kenyan population live below the poverty line.

This translates to over eight million people in the country with no access to Healthcare due to lack of funds.




Our progress

Okoa Maisha helps the needy people in Kenya pay for hospital bills.






Our Mission is to create social change through provision of healthcare to all.

A healthy population results in a productive nation. We hope to restore hope for the future which will make families, communities and the nation at large thrive.





How It Works

Patients or their relatives contact us using our telephone lines or send us an email. We then contact them back and plan a meet up where they bring their hospital documentation for authentication.

Donors who want to remain anonymous can send funds to our cause by clicking the donate button. We also have an option where the donor meets the patient and they get to know each other before they pay the hospital bills for them directly to the hospital.
